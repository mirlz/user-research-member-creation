Noah Chong

# of players: 50 ~ 100

Profile

Noah is a part-time agent who have a small pool of players under him, as he has a full time sales job that provides a more flexible working hours. This enable him to manage his current players during his off time, usually he get new players from word of mouth by his players.

Since he does not have much time managing, he is satisfied with his current amount of players, and does not work on actively recruiting more players as that will eat into his full time job and resting hours. However, as he has a full time job, he would usually be more on the go during working hours and thus would not be stationary most of the time.
