Improving player creation user flow process

Brief: To design a better system or workflow where users can:

  • Login quicker
  • Browse / navigate easier regardless of type of devices used
  • Create player account(s) quicker
  • Allow for easier passing of player account(s) details to players

Who are our users?

Brief: Our users are the agents, including MA, SMA, or VVIPs possibly who uses our agent site for:-

  • Manage downline (members / players) accounts' credit / balance / commission
  • Transfer downline balance
  • Checking reports (winloss / statement etc)
  • Member account(s) creation

Personas


Noah Chong

Age: 33
Residence: Indonesia, Jakarta
# of players: 50 ~ 100
Marital status: Single | No children
"I am always on the go, so I pretty much do everything on either my phone or my tablet."

Profile

Noah is a part-time agent who have a small pool of players under him, as he has a full time sales job that provides a more flexible working hours. This enable him to manage his current players during his off time, usually he get new players from word of mouth by his players.

Since he does not have much time managing, he is satisfied with his current amount of players, and does not work on actively recruiting more players as that will eat into his full time job and resting hours. However, as he has a full time job, he would usually be more on the go during working hours and thus would not be stationary most of the time.
